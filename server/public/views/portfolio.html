Sassy Scheduler

I developed an application to simplify planning adventures for the Sassy Pants Bicycle Riding Collective.
Previously, we were trying to coordinate schedules via text, email and social media. I created a simple interaction where members could schedule an outing, and view all upcoming adventures.
Technologies used: PostgreSQL, Express.js, AngularJS, Node.js, Angular Material, Passport



Mango Grove

Mango is a free, online toolkit to help people connect and build their networks for useful career advice.
Mango is a startup, and asked us to build a place where users could post shout-outs, wins (called Mango Moments) and affirmations.
Collaborated with a group of four other developers to build Mango Grove, an easy-to-use application that allowed Mango to build
their community by allowing users to connect with a messaging system that would build positive feedback loops, as well as nurture and grow new networkers to help them along their chosen paths.
Mango Grove allows early adopters to share their personal successes and encourage other users to try Mango Grove, too.
Technologies used: MongoDB, Express.js, Node.js, JQuery, Bootstrap
